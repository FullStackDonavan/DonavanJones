## Who This Is For

You want to run AI models on your own hardware — for cost control, privacy, or just to understand how the pieces actually fit together — instead of wiring every feature through a third-party API. This starter kit is the practical layer underneath the [AI Engineering series](/categories/ai-engineering): the setup steps, diagrams, and code that turn "I have a GPU" into "I have a working local inference stack."

## Why It's Different From Just Reading the Blog

The blog series explains the reasoning and the failure modes. This kit is the working starting point — the Ollama commands, the pipeline templates, and the FastAPI scaffolding you'd otherwise have to assemble yourself from a dozen different docs pages.
